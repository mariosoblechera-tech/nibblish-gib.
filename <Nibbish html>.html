Our Story

BORN FROM
PASSION

Nibblish by Itané brings the best of Roman street food culture to the heart of Gibraltar. We believe food should be an experience — bold, authentic, and made with love.

From our 72-hour fermented pinsa dough to our handmade supplì and arancine, every dish is crafted with the finest Italian ingredients, fused with the vibrant flavours of our community.

OUR

Also available as Smiles — open-faced pinsa bases with your favourite toppings, made with the same extraordinary dough matured for 72 hours.
